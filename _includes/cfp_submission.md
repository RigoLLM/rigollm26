**Formatting.** Submissions must use the [ACM `acmart` article template](https://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template)
in the two-column `sigconf` format, 10-point font
(`\documentclass[sigconf,anonymous,review]{acmart}`). Page limits above exclude
references. Papers may include an appendix; there is **no page limit for the appendix**,
but reviewers are not obligated to read it.

**Double-blind review.** Reviewing is double-blind. Submissions must not reveal the
identity of the authors: omit author names and affiliations, refer to your own prior work
in the third person, and anonymize links to code, data, or supplementary material.

**Deadlines.** All deadlines are 11:59 PM AoE (Anywhere on Earth).
